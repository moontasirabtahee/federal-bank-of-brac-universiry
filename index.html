The role of banks in our economy is important because they provide a safe and convenient way for individuals and businesses to save and borrow money, and to make and receive payments. Banks also play a crucial role in supporting the growth and stability of the economy by providing credit and financial services to households and businesses. Without banks, it would be much more difficult for people and companies to access the financial resources they need to invest, grow, and prosper.
